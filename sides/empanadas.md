# Meat Empanadas Recipe

## Ingredients

- 500g ground beef
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
- 1/2 cup green olives, sliced
- 1/2 cup red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 package empanada dough discs (or homemade)
- 1 egg (for egg wash)

## Instructions

- Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
- Add onion, garlic, and red bell pepper, sauté until soft
- Add ground beef, cook until browned
- Stir in cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper
- Add chopped hard-boiled eggs and green olives, mix well
- Let filling cool
- Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each dough disc
- Fold and seal the edges with a fork
- Brush with beaten egg
- Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown
- Enjoy!
